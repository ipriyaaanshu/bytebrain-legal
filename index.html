The problem

Contract review is broken

Every week, founders, attorneys, and CPAs sign contracts that cost them money — because manual review is slow, expensive, and misses things.

Missed clauses that cost you

Auto-renewal traps. One-sided IP assignments. Liability caps you didn't see. Manual review misses them — especially when you're rushing to close.

2–4 hours per document

That's a founder's afternoon. That's $400–$800 of attorney time. For a routine NDA review. Multiplied across every vendor, partner, and hire.

AI hallucinations are worse

General AI tools cite clauses that don't exist. Lawyers have been sanctioned for AI-generated citations. Confident wrong answers are more dangerous than slow right ones.

How it works

Three steps. Under 30 seconds.

No training. No setup. Upload and go.

1

Upload your contract

PDF, DOCX, or plain text. NDA, vendor agreement, employment contract, SAFE — the agent auto-detects the document type.

2

Agent analyzes in seconds

Why ByteBrain

Built differently from Ivo and LexIQ

We're not a Word plugin. We're not enterprise CLM software. We're an API-first contract intelligence agent built for speed, accuracy, and trust.

Exact clause citations

Every finding references the exact section and page number. You can verify any finding in 10 seconds. No fabricated citations, ever.

Immutable audit trail

Every analysis is timestamped and hashed. You always know what was reviewed, when, and what the agent found. Legal defensibility built in.

API-first design

Not locked to Word or Salesforce. Call our REST API from any system — CRM, document management, email, or your own internal tools. Embed anywhere.

Detects what's missing

Most tools only find risky clauses. We also surface missing standard terms — no mutual NDA provisions, no IP carve-outs, no limitation of liability. The gap is often the risk.

Self-hosted deployment

Attorney-client privilege matters. With self-hosted deployment, your documents are processed locally — never sent to our servers. Full data control for law firms and CPAs.

Extensible skill system

Add custom analysis skills for your domain: GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, or your firm's specific playbook. Legal today, finance tomorrow, compliance next week.
